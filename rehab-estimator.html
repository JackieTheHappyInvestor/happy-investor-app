Here's How This Works!
1
Enter your property info at the top. Add the address, bedrooms, bathrooms and square footage. If you're planning to ADD a bedroom or bathroom, go ahead and increase the number — the estimate will adjust automatically!
2
Toggle ON everything that needs fixing. Leave OFF what's already good. The estimate updates live as you go!
3
See your estimate instantly. If you already have a number in mind, enter it in the pink box and the estimate steps aside automatically. Your number takes over!
4
Keep this number to yourself! Use it as YOUR starting point before meeting with contractors. Never share your estimate with a contractor before they give you their quote first!
